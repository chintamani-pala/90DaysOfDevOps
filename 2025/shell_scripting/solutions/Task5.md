## Why this matters for DevOps?
- **Usability:** Well-documented tools are easier to use and maintain. A help menu is a standard requirement for any command-line utility.
- **Onboarding:** New team members can quickly understand how to use internal tools without needing external documentation.
- **Error Prevention:** Clear instructions on expected inputs and flags reduce the likelihood of destructive mistakes.
